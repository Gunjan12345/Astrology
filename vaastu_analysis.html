Home > Services > Vaastu Analysis

According to the ancient Vaastu Vidya, there are certain geographical and astrological calculations that must be kept in mind, while constructing a building. At times, certain structures create negative energy due to poorly thought layout thus leading to predicaments in terms of loss of wealth, health, peace, and problems like family conflicts, marital difficulties, etc. Vaastu is the Indian science of directions, and it teaches us how to optimize on the various directions and achieve the best of everything, simply by occupying space in the most favorable way. As much as sun rays affect everybody, Vaastu shastra principles are also causing their influence on everyone without distinction - such as rich or poor, Indian or American, do-gooders or evildoers, children or adults, males or females, irrespective of caste and community. The idea behind this philosophy is that the constructed space has to vibrate with the individual spirit of the dweller. There is a continuous interaction between positive and negative cosmic forces. At Shakti-Sutra, we provide the most accurate analysis of how your abode must be constructed. We provide simple guidelines for you to follow to ensure the happiness and prosperity in your family life, work/business, etc.
